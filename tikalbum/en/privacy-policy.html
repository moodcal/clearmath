TikAlbum Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

Welcome to TikAlbum. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we handle your personal information. TikAlbum is a completely offline video playback application, and we take your privacy protection very seriously.

1. Information Access and Usage

1.1. Local Storage Access: TikAlbum only accesses video files on your device for video playback functionality.

1.2. We do not collect or transmit any personal data to our servers or third parties.

2. Required Permissions

The application requires the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media Access Permission: To read video files from your device

3. Data Security

3.1. TikAlbum is a completely offline application with no network functionality.

3.2. Your video content always remains stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to the cloud or shared with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted in the app or on our website.
